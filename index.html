The Story Behind the Song

Inspired by Luke 1–2, “A Mother’s Cry” follows Mary’s encounter with the angel Gabriel, the encounter of the angels and the shepherds on the night of Jesus' birth, and the heavens’ triumphant chorus:

“Glory to God in the highest!”
